AZORES

Introduction

This project is the result of a casual trip to the Azores in 2019, where I had the opportunity to explore and capture the unparalleled beauty of the island of São Miguel through the lens of my camera.

About Azores

Filled with fascinating and enchanting landscapes, the island of São Miguel is a true paradise on earth. Its diverse scenery, ranging from volcanic lakes and green mountains to stunning beaches and picturesque villages, offers a visual spectacle at every corner.

Project Objective

The goal of this project is to share the essence and magic of São Miguel, transporting the viewer to this natural paradise through carefully captured images. Each photograph aims not only to portray the island's natural beauty but also to convey the tranquility and authenticity that make it unique.
